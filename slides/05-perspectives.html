03 / PERSPECTIVES
CASE ANALYSIS SOLUTION REFS
WHO SEES WHAT

Three perspectives. One broken system.

01
HR Director
"We're losing credibility."
Appraisals must be defensible. Without documented criteria and calibration, every promotion invites a grievance. HR sees legal risk, turnover costs, and a broken talent pipeline.
02
CEO / Executive
"This hits the bottom line."
Biased reviews = wrong people promoted = wrong bets on talent. Disengagement cuts productivity; high performers leave. Fairness is a business strategy, not just a policy.
03
Employee
"Why should I even try?"
If politics beats performance, the effort/reward contract is broken. Top performers quietly quit — or actually quit. Trust, once lost, doesn't come back with one memo.
