WHY SINGLE-TRACK FAILS

Neither option alone is enough.

A
OPTION A
Retrain only
Fixes the how, not the what. If the system itself is vague — no clear KRAs, subjective scales, no calibration — even well-trained managers produce biased output. You're polishing a broken instrument.
B
OPTION B
Redesign only
A new system run by untrained managers creates new bias with better paperwork. Employees lose trust twice — once in the old system, again in the rollout that didn't fix anything.
