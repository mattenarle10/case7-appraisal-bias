CASE ANALYSIS SOLUTION REFS
THE SEQUENCED PLAN

A 12-month rollout across three phases.

  • 01
    0–3 MONTHS
    Retrain + add guardrails

    Calibration sessions (managers defend ratings to peers). Mandatory evidence for every score. Unconscious bias training. Quick win to restore trust while the bigger fix is built.

  • 02
    3–9 MONTHS
    Redesign the system

    Shift to measurable KPIs tied to role outcomes. Introduce 360° feedback to dilute single-manager bias. Publish promotion criteria. Separate performance review from compensation talks.

  • 03
    9–12 MONTHS
    Institutionalize

    HR analytics monitoring rating distributions by manager — flag outliers. Appeals channel. Annual audit of promotion decisions against performance data. Publish aggregate results.
